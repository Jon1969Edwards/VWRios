Under the GDPR, we must have a legal basis to process your personal data. Our legal bases are:

    Where you have given your consent to process your data for specific purposes.

    Processing necessary for our legitimate interests, provided these interests are not overridden by your data protection rights.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

6.3 Restriction of Processing

6.4 Objection to Processing
